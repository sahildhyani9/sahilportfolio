BOBCARD

Year

2022 - 2022

BOBCARD, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Bank of Baroda, set an ambitious goal to significantly increase its credit card acquisition rate. This strategic initiative reflects the brand's commitment to expanding its customer base, enhancing market penetration, and strengthening its competitive positioning in the evolving financial services and credit card landscape. The objective was aligned with broader organizational goals of digital adoption, customer-centricity, and financial inclusion.

Challenges

  • Building brand awareness and credibility in a crowded market
  • Competing with well-established players
  • Operating with limited resources compared to larger competitors

Solution

Part 1: Driving Product Awareness

  • Tailored messaging for different products
  • A/B testing with diverse creative assets

Part 2: Supporting DIY Journey

  • Multiple digital touchpoints
  • Retargeting and reminders

Awareness Strategy

  • Integrated channels (digital, WhatsApp, Google Ads)
  • Customized messaging & automation

Result

  • 60% acquisition increase from March to December
  • Improved customer experience via personalized messaging
