QS

Year

2019 - 2019

QS is one of the largest high education advisory system in the World, known for University ranking. QS provides the insights and tools to learners for exploring possibilities toward a specific country, university, or program.

Challenges

  • Targeting Decision-Makers: The primary goal was to attract participants from the leadership of higher education institutions, including university chancellors, policy makers, and senior administrators.
  • Niche Audience with Limited Digital Activity: Engaging academic leadership—a segment that’s often less active on mainstream digital platforms—posed a challenge for outreach and conversion.
  • High-Value Event Ticketing: Convincing prospects to register for a premium offline event ($1800+ ticket) with no previous precedent at this scale by QS added to the acquisition difficulty.

Solution

Integrated Full-Funnel Marketing Strategy:

  • LinkedIn Ads targeted by job titles (Vice Chancellors, Deans, Directors) and sectors (Education, Government).
  • Facebook Ads were used to support mid-funnel retargeting and community awareness.
  • In-system communication including QS’s owned database and email sequences for lead nurturing.
  • Account-Based Marketing (ABM): Focused on high-priority institutions with personalized communication and outreach playbooks.

Credibility-First Messaging:

  • Emphasized QS’s global authority in higher education and curated content highlighting industry relevance.
  • Highlighted peer participation, partnerships, and session value to build trust and urgency.

Performance Optimization:

  • Used A/B testing to refine creatives and messaging based on region and institution type.
  • Adjusted bidding and timing strategy to align with university budget approval cycles and academic calendars.

Result

High-Value Registration Success:

  • 1100+ paid registrations secured for the leadership event—exceeding campaign targets.
  • First time QS executed an offline event of this scale targeting education leadership, demonstrating strong market validation.

Cost Efficiency & ROI: Achieved an efficient $230 CPA against a $1800 average ticket price, resulting in an ROI of nearly 7.8x per ticket.

Strategic Business Outcome:

  • Campaign success led to increased internal confidence in scaling leadership summits across global education hubs.
  • Opened a new revenue stream and audience segment for QS's events vertical.
