Teikametrics

Year

2022 - 2022

Teikametrics helps sellers and brand owners optimize performance across top marketplaces and industry verticals. With AI-driven technology and optional managed services, it removes manual overhead—enabling businesses to focus on what truly matters: creating products and experiences that delight their customers.

Challenges

  • Targeting High-Value Leads: The core objective was to acquire Marketing Qualified Leads (MQLs) and Sales Qualified Leads (SQLs) from businesses generating over $1 million in annual revenue on global marketplaces like Amazon, eBay, Flipkart, etc.
  • Unstructured Demand Signals: The lack of a clearly defined audience profile and inconsistent data across channels made targeting and segmentation complex and imprecise.
  • Fuzzy Buyer Persona: Limited insight into purchasing behavior, decision-making cycles, and pain points slowed down campaign optimization and increased acquisition costs.

Solution

Integrated Demand Generation Strategy:

  • Ran LinkedIn Ads targeting job titles and industries mapped to marketplace sellers.
  • Launched Facebook Ads for mid-funnel awareness and lead nurturing through retargeting.
  • Deployed Google Search Ads with high-intent keyword targeting to capture bottom-funnel traffic.

Refined Lead Qualification Framework:

  • Built custom landing pages with industry-specific messaging and lead scoring models.
  • Applied marketing automation to nurture and qualify leads, improving MQL accuracy and SQL conversion over time.

Data-Driven Optimization:

  • Conducted continuous A/B testing on creatives and CTAs.
  • Optimized bidding strategies across platforms to balance cost-per-acquisition (CPA) with lead quality.

Result

Lead Generation & Conversion:

  • 800+ leads generated, with 23% qualifying as MQLs — a strong indicator of strategic targeting.
  • 5.3% SQL conversion rate sustained over the first quarter, validating campaign maturity and improved pipeline quality.

Strong ROI Metrics:

  • Maintained an average $520 CPA while supporting a $12,000 Lifetime Value (LTV) per customer — resulting in highly profitable acquisition.
  • Overall lead-to-customer pipeline performance improved by 230% in efficiency year over year.
