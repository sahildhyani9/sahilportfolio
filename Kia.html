KIA Motors India

Year

2018 - 2018

KIA Motors, a sister company of Hyundai, set its sights on entering the competitive Indian automobile market with a bold debut. The objective was to make a strong first impression at the Auto Expo 2018 — India’s largest automotive exhibition — by showcasing the brand’s identity, technological prowess, and an exciting lineup of vehicles tailored for Indian consumers. This strategic move was designed not only to introduce KIA as a premium yet accessible automotive brand, but also to build brand awareness, generate market buzz, and lay the foundation for long-term growth in one of the world’s fastest-growing car markets.

Challenges

  • Low Brand Awareness: As a new entrant in the Indian automobile market, KIA faced the challenge of introducing an unfamiliar brand to a diverse and competitive audience.
  • Established Competition: The market was already saturated with well-recognized automotive brands with loyal customer bases and deeper market penetration.
  • Resource Constraints: Compared to industry incumbents, the brand had relatively limited local presence and operational infrastructure at launch.

Solution

  • Integrated Multi-Channel Marketing: Leveraged a mix of digital marketing, print ads, banners, standees, and out-of-home media to generate high visibility across touchpoints.
  • Digital & Direct Engagement: Ran highly targeted campaigns on WhatsApp and Google Ads, enabling personalized messaging and higher recall.
  • Content & Creative Strategy: Created diverse creatives and messaging, backed by continuous A/B testing to identify the most effective combinations.
  • Experiential Engagement at Auto Expo 2018:
    • Designed interactive booth experiences to attract visitors.
    • Organized contests and engagement activities to increase footfall and drive interest in the brand’s vehicle lineup.
    • Distributed branded merchandise and real-time social media coverage to enhance virality and on-ground buzz.

Result

  • Massive Brand Visibility:
    • Total Reach: Over 3 million (30,48,262) individuals exposed to the campaign.
    • Total Impressions: Over 6 million (62,34,267) across various media channels between March and December.
  • Rapid Community Growth: Achieved 100,000+ follower acquisition across all social media platforms — building an engaged digital audience in less than a year.
  • Strong Debut Impact: Successfully established KIA as a new and credible automotive brand in India, paving the way for future launches and sustained engagement.
